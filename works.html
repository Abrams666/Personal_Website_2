Class Manager

After experiencing high school life for more than a year, I found that there are many scattered, messy, and easy-to-forget class tasks in the class, which are often ignored because different teachers announce them in different ways, such as: ordering meals, announcing homework, arranging exams... ....etc., once someone forgets, it will easily reduce the operating efficiency of the entire class and even the school. So I wanted to use a platform to give everyone different permissions and manage each other to integrate these messy class tasks.
